Overview

Gustaf Almenberg is based in Stockholm, Sweden. His work spans art, psychotherapy, psychiatry, healthcare systems, leadership dynamics, and interdisciplinary inquiry.

Work in Progress

  • An integrated reconceptualization of mental health complementing DSM and ICD models.
  • Ongoing research on the dynamics at the intersection of psychology, psychiatry, philosophy, and theology.

Present Areas of Interest

Participatory Art
Developments in the theory behind participatory art as well as participatory approaches in sculpture and photography.
Reconceptualising Psychiatry
Toward a more integrated framework complementing DSM and ICD models.
Healthcare Systems
Organizational models for national healthcare and systemic reform.
Interdisciplinary Dynamics
Work at the intersection of psychology, psychiatry, philosophy, and theology.
