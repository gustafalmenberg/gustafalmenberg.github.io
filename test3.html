Overview

Gustaf Almenberg is based in Stockholm, Sweden.

Family: Erik Almenberg and Johan Almenberg.

Present Areas of Interest

  • Participatory Art – Developments in the theory behind participatory art as well as the development of participatory approaches in sculpture and photography.
  • Reconceptualising Psychiatry – Toward a more integrated framework complementing DSM and ICD models.
  • Healthcare Systems – Organizational models for national healthcare and systemic reform.
  • Interdisciplinary Dynamics – Work at the intersection of psychology, psychiatry, philosophy, and theology.
