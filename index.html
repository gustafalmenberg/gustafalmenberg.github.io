Overview

Gustaf Almenberg is based in Stockholm, Sweden. His work spans dynamic systems thinking, participatory art, and interdisciplinary exploration across psychiatry, philosophy, and healthcare systems.

Areas of Interest

  • Participatory Art – Development of participatory approaches in sculpture and photography.
  • Reconceptualising Psychiatry – Toward a more integrated framework complementing DSM and ICD models.
  • Healthcare Systems – Organizational models for national healthcare and systemic reform.
  • Interdisciplinary Dynamics – Work at the intersection of psychology, psychiatry, philosophy, and theology.
