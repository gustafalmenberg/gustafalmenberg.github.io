About Gustaf Almenberg

Gustaf Almenberg is based in Stockholm, Sweden, with work spanning participatory art, psychiatry, psychotherapy, healthcare systems, and interdisciplinary inquiry.

Present Areas of Interest

Participatory ArtThe theory behind participatory art, sculpture, and photography.
Reconceptualising PsychiatryAn integrated framework complementing DSM and ICD models.
Healthcare SystemsOrganizational models for national healthcare and systemic reform.
Interdisciplinary DynamicsPsychology, psychiatry, philosophy, and theology.
